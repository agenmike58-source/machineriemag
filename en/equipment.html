Materials

Materials we machine

We work the four most common alloys in industrial mechanical manufacturing.

Fe

Carbon & Alloy Steel

1018, 4140, 4340, A36. Structural parts, shafts, gears, bolts, production tooling.

Al

Aluminum

6061-T6, 7075-T6, 2024. Light weight and excellent machinability — aerospace components, housings, plates.

SS

303, 304, 316L. Corrosion resistance for food processing, medical and harsh environments.

Cu

Brass & Copper

C360, C110. Excellent conductivity and machinability — fittings, bushings, electrical components.
