What we do

A complete shop,
one address.

From drawing to finished part, our team handles the full machining chain. Short runs, hard-to-find replacement parts or recurring production — we hold tight tolerances and meet your deadlines.

01

CNC Machining

3- and 4-axis machining centres for complex parts in aluminum, steel and stainless. CAM programming from your files or drawings.

02

Turning

Conventional and CNC turning for shafts, bushings, bolts and cylindrical precision parts, from prototype to production run.

03

Milling

Precision milling for jigs, moulds, brackets and custom mechanical components — complex surfaces and multi-hole patterns.

04

TIG / MIG Welding

Assembly and repair welding on steel, aluminum and stainless steel. Certified welders for structural and precision work.

05

Industrial Repair

Restoration and reproduction of broken or obsolete equipment parts — we re-make what your supplier no longer produces.

06

Prototyping

From concept to first functional part: prototypes and pre-production runs to validate your designs before full production.
