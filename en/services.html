Our services

Six disciplines,
one shop.

From single prototype to production run, Machinerie MAG covers the full machining chain — from CAM programming through final dimensional inspection.

CNC 3 & 4 axis
01 / 06

CNC Machining

Our 3- and 4-axis machining centres produce complex parts from your CAD files or 2D drawings. Integrated CAM programming, precision setup and in-process inspection on every operation.

  • 3- and 4-axis machining centres (Haas, Mazak)
  • CAM programming from STEP, IGES, DXF files
  • Materials: steel, aluminum, stainless, brass, engineering plastics
  • Max. travel: 1200 × 600 × 600 mm
  • Standard tolerance ±0.01 mm — tight tolerance available
  • Short runs, recurring production, one-off parts
Ø 0–450 mm
02 / 06

CNC Turning

CNC and conventional lathes for shafts, bushings, bolts, cones and all rotational parts. From prototype to long production run, with continuous dimensional control.

  • CNC and conventional lathes, capacity Ø 0–450 mm
  • Max. between-centres length: 1,500 mm
  • Shafts, bushings, bolts, cones, cylindrical parts
  • Internal and external threading (metric, NPT, UNC)
  • Steel, aluminum, stainless, brass, bronze
  • Surface finish Ra 0.8 µm available
Travel 1200 × 600 mm
03 / 06

Precision Milling

Vertical and horizontal milling for jigs, moulds, brackets, plates and complex mechanical components. Tapped holes, slots, contours — surfaces and drilling patterns to the hundredth.

  • CNC and conventional vertical mills
  • Production jigs, fixtures and tooling
  • Moulds and forming dies
  • Custom brackets, plates and frames
  • Keyways, dovetails, T-slots, grooves
  • Drilling, boring and tapping in series
TIG · MIG · Steel · SS · Alu
04 / 06

TIG / MIG Welding

Assembly and repair welding on all common metals. Our certified welders handle both structural joints and fine-bead work on stainless steel or aluminum.

  • TIG welding (GTAW) — stainless, aluminum, carbon steel
  • MIG/MAG welding (GMAW) — mild and galvanized steel
  • Frame, base plate and structural assemblies
  • Repair and hard-facing of worn parts
  • Certified welders — fine and structural work
  • Straightening, fixturing and distortion control
Reproduction & restoration
05 / 06

Industrial Repair

Broken part, obsolete component, discontinued supplier — we re-make what nobody else produces. Exact reproduction, weld build-up and restoration to original dimensions.

  • Reverse engineering and exact reproduction
  • Restoration to original dimensions by build-up welding
  • Shafts, bushings, pulleys, gears — refurbished and remachined
  • Hard-to-find replacement parts fabricated on demand
  • Fast turnaround — 24 h emergency service on call
  • Documentation and traceability on all work orders
From concept to part

Prototyping & Pre-production

From concept to first functional part, we help you validate designs before committing to full production. Single prototypes to pre-production runs of 5 to 50 pieces.

  • Functional prototypes from CAD files
  • Pre-production runs of 5–50 parts for validation
  • Machinability feedback and design revision support
  • Fast turnaround — first prototypes in 3–5 business days
  • Dimensional inspection report provided
  • Seamless transition to production run, no added cost
